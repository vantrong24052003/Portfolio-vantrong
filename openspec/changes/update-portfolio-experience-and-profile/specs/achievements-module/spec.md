## ADDED Requirements

### Requirement: Ruby Silver Certification Badge
The system SHALL provide a visually distinct badge for the Ruby Silver Certification.

#### Scenario: Achievement Recognition
- **WHEN** the Identity Module is rendered
- **THEN** it SHALL display a golden-themed "Ruby Silver Certificate" badge with a trophy or gemstone icon and a subtle glow effect.

### Requirement: Professional Experience Badge
The system SHALL prominently display the developer's years of experience.

#### Scenario: Experience Verification
- **WHEN** the Overview scene loads
- **THEN** it SHALL show a "1 Year Experience" indicator with a high-tech border and primary color accent.
